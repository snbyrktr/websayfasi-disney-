History

Disney Brand History… We are here with a brand history article in which we all admire our favorite characters from A to Z. While we talk about the establishment of Disney, that is, the history of the brand, try to identify your favorite character. The brand we know as Disney today is actually Walt Disney himself. Walt Disney was born in 1901 in Chicago. His father was very ill, so the family's livelihood depended on his work. Moreover, since Walt did not have a specific profession, it was not easy to find a job. He earned money from many jobs, from newspaper delivery to ambulance driver. He was earning money, but at this time his father's condition was getting worse and worse. As a matter of fact, Walt lost his father and he constantly blamed himself for it. He thought that if he had made more money, maybe his father would not have died. While Walt's accusations continued, he came across an advertisement and, with the light of his inspiration, attended free art trainings. Although he made drawings for many agencies, he was not successful and could not make any money. He decided to go to Hollywood and try himself. Although he made many different drawings, he still did not manage to earn money and continued to work at night.

Disney Brand History Revolution & Mouse

He often encountered a tiny mouse in the warehouse where he worked at night. She was giving him a piece of his bread and watching it. One day, inspiration came to W.Disney here and inspired by the movements of this mouse, he painted the character we all know closely, namely Mickey Mouse. Here is the last invention of the moneyless days came from the Mickey Mouse pen.

It should be noted that Walt painted countless characters in the 10 years he came to Hollywood and became the father of these characters. Although the end of the penniless and bad course was shaped by an advertisement, it ended in Hollywood. Even though the story of the Disney brand developed in this way, Disney's heroes will remain as the best memories of our childhood. So who is your Disney hero? Mickey Mouse?
